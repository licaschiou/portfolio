ABOUT

Hi, I am Chiu Wei-Chieh, a creative director and interactive designer based in Taipei. I communicate concepts through playful, meaningful ways, compelling story and using interactive elements to create engagement.

I have been worked for electronic manufacturer and exhibition design company as interactive designer and joined a digital agency as co-owner and creative director. Currently I am freelance and always looking for next challenge.

Beside working, I love gaming, spending time on online courses and experimenting ideas on Processing. Thanks for stopping by.

This site is created with lots of help from internet. To give something back, I put the source code of this site on github.
