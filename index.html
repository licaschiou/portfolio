About me

Hi, I am Chiu Wei-Chieh, a creative director and interactive designer based in Taipei.

I design to create engagement and i like to do it with a touch of gamification, to communicate concepts through playful, meaningful ways and compelling story.

I have been worked for electronic manufacturer and exhibition design company as interactive designer and joined a digital agency as co-owner and creative director. Currently I am freelance and always looking for next challenge.

Beside working, I love gaming, spending time on various online courses and experimenting ideas on Processing. Thanks for stopping by.
